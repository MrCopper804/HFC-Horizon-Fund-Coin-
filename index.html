Six Simple Steps

HFC Exchange simplifies complex multi-sig trade workflows into a highly secure, easy-to-follow trading protocol.

1

Create Account

Register your private trading node. Input email verification parameters to activate your standard user profile.

2

Deposit PKR

Deposit PKR funds instantly into your secure local wallet module. PKR serves as the trading bridge for crypto buys.

3
